The San Gabriel Mountains are a mountain range located in Los Angeles County, California. They are part of the Transverse Ranges and stretch from the Cajon Pass in the east to the Newhall Pass in the west. The highest peak in the range is Mount San Antonio, also known as Mount Baldy, which stands at 10,069 feet (3,069 meters).
